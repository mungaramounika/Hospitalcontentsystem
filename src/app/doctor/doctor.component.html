Elizabeth Blackwell

Elizabeth Blackwell is one of those extraordinary people whose careers seem to sum up what makes New York so unique and so great.The nation's first woman medical doctor, she reminds us that Gotham is one of the world's dynamos of medical innovation,the place where hypodermic syringes were first used—at Bellevue in 1856—and the first appendectomy performed—at Roosevelt Hospital in 1886.
